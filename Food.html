Best Food and Dishes of Ireland..!

These are some of my favourite Irish Foods.!

Traditional Irish Breakfast..!

"Full Irish" breakfast consists of sausages, rashers (bacon), eggs pudding, white pudding and toast.!

Often there will be a grilled tomato as well as mushrooms, and baked beans.

and of course, no breakfast is complete without a lovely pot of tea

Bangers and Mash..!

The classic of sausages, mashed potato and gravy is not unique to ireland, but what makes it special is the Irish sausages. Most countries have their own way of making sausages, and they are one thing I miss from home if I'm away travelling.!
